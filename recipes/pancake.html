Delicious Pancakes

Description

To make pancakes, get ready your eggs, flour and water or milk. Depending on the amount of batter, you can prepare them in just 15 minutes. You require a good pan and a plastic spatula.

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • sugar
  • salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a jug
  2. Add the dry ingredients
  3. Mix well
  4. Add the eggs and the milk
  5. Use your beater to properly mix the batter
  6. Put 1 tsp of oil on the pan, when it's hot pour some batter on moderate heat
  7. When it's nicely brown, turn it
  8. Add 1 tsp oil and cook till it's brown
  9. Follow the same procedure for the remaining batter
  10. Serve hot with tea or coffee
