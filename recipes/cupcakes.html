Cupcakes

Description

Cupcakes are a great treat for children and a yummy dessert or accompaniment to the 4 o'clock tea. It's quite straightforward to prepare them. And you can flavour them to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cp milk
  • 1 cp butter
  • grated rind of 1 lemon
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cp sugar
  • half tsp salt

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  2. Add flour that's mixed with salt and baking powder interchangeably with an egg each time till all eggs are used up
  3. Now add the mixed flour alternately with the milk until the milk is used up
  4. Fold in the remaining flour
  5. Add the grated lemon
  6. Pour your mixture into a cupcake baking tray andd bake at 350 degrees F
