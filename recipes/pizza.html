Pizza

Description

Pizza is a quick and easy food that can be eaten at any time of day. What's more, you can add toppings you like to make it very tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1kg baking flour
  • 3tsps yeast
  • 3tbsps sugar
  • 3tbsps oil
  • 1l warm water
  • half tsp salt
  • toppings
  • grated cheese

Steps

  1. Place your flour in a bowl
  2. Add the yeast, sugar and salt
  3. Mix well then add oil and water
  4. Thoroughly knead it
  5. Cover it with a damp cloth or cling film
  6. Let it rest for 1 to 2 hours
  7. Knead it again to remove excess air
  8. Roll out and spread your toppings
  9. Sprinkle the cheese evenly
  10. Bake on medium heat for 30 to 45 minutes
  11. Serve immediately
